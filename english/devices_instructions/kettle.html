Kettle Instructions

Filling the Kettle

  • Remove the kettle from its base before filling it.
  • Fill the kettle with the desired amount of water, ensuring it is between the indicated minimum and maximum levels.
  • Do not overfill the kettle as boiling water may spill out.
  • Ensure the lid is properly closed.

Using the Kettle

  • Place the kettle on its base and plug it into the power outlet.
  • Press the power button to start the kettle.
  • The kettle will automatically turn off when the water reaches boiling point. You can also manually stop the boiling before removing it from the base to pour the water.

After Use

  • Unplug the kettle and let it cool down before cleaning or storing it.
  • Do not leave water in the kettle after use as it accelerates limescale formation.

Cleaning and Maintenance

  • Unplug the kettle before cleaning it.
  • Clean the exterior with a damp sponge. Do not immerse the kettle, its base, the cord, or the plug in water.
  • Regularly clean the filter. If the filter is clogged, it can be cleaned under a tap or with a brush. For descaling, use white vinegar or citric acid as recommended in the manual.

Safety Precautions

  • Do not use the kettle without water to avoid damage.
  • Be careful of the hot steam coming out of the spout.
  • Only touch the kettle by its handle to avoid burns from the hot body.
  • Keep the appliance and its cord out of reach of children under 8 years old.
  • Never open the lid when the water is boiling.
  • Do not use the kettle if the power cord or plug is damaged.

Troubleshooting

  • If the kettle does not work or stops before boiling, check that it is properly connected and ensure there is no limescale buildup. Let the kettle cool down and refill it with water if necessary.
  • For any anomalies or if the kettle does not work properly, unplug it and contact the host, Alessandro.
