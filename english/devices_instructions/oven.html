Oven functions

  • Off: to turn off the oven.

  • Light: to turn on the cavity light.

  • Static: for cooking any type of dish on a single shelf. It is preferable to use the 2nd level.

  • Diamond Clean: the action of steam released during this special low-temperature cleaning cycle allows easy removal of dirt and food residues.

  • Fan: for baking cakes with liquid filling on a single shelf. This function can also be used for baking on two shelves.

  • Grill: for grilling steaks, kebabs, sausages, gratinating vegetables, or toasting bread.

  • Turbo Grill: for roasting large pieces of meat (legs, roast beef, chickens).

  • Defrosting: to speed up the defrosting of food.

Daily use

  • Select a function: turn the selection knob to the symbol of the desired function.
  • Start a manual function: turn the thermostat knob to set the desired temperature.
  • Preheating: the thermostat LED on indicates that the preheating phase is active.
  • Set the timer: turn the timer knob fully clockwise and then back to the desired duration, proceeding counterclockwise.

Cooking table

  • Leavened cakes: 150 - 170°C for 30 - 90 minutes on level 2.
  • Filled cakes: 160 - 200°C for 35 - 90 minutes on level 2.
  • Cookies/Cupcakes: 160 - 180°C for 15 - 35 minutes on level 2 or 3.
  • Pizza / Focaccia: 220 - 250°C for 10 - 25 minutes on level 1 or 2.
  • Lasagna / Baked pasta: 190 - 200°C for 45 - 65 minutes on level 2.
  • Pork roast with crackling: 180 - 190°C for 110 - 150 minutes on level 2.

Care and cleaning

  • Clean external surfaces with a damp microfiber cloth.
  • Clean internal surfaces after each use, letting the oven cool down.
  • Activate the “Diamond Clean” function for optimal cleaning of internal surfaces.
  • Soak accessories with dishwashing detergent after use.

Useful tips

  • Use the “Fan” function to cook different foods on different shelves simultaneously.
  • Use dark metal accessories or pyrex or ceramic cookware for longer cooking times.
