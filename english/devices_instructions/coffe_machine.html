Coffee machine instructions

Preparation

  • Rinse and fill the water tank with drinking water.
  • Turn on the machine by pressing the Espresso or Lungo button.
  • The lights will blink while heating (about 25 seconds) and stay steady when the machine is ready.

Making coffee

  • Lift the lever and insert a Nespresso capsule.
  • Close the lever completely and place a cup under the dispenser.
  • Press the Espresso (40 ml) or Lungo (110 ml) button.
  • The coffee will be dispensed automatically. You can stop or increase the dispensing by pressing the Espresso/Lungo button again.

Removing the capsule

  • Lift and close the lever to eject the used capsule into the capsule container.

Safety precautions

  • Do not lift the lever during coffee dispensing.
  • Do not insert fingers under the spout or into the capsule compartment.
  • Use only drinking water and do not leave water in the tank for more than two days.
  • Turn off the machine by pressing both the Espresso and Lungo buttons simultaneously.
