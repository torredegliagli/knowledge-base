Air conditioner instructions

Using the remote control

  • Handle with care, avoid getting it wet or dropping it.
  • Do not expose to direct sunlight.
  • Remove the batteries if not used for a long period.
  • Keep the remote control in a position where the signal can reach the device's receiver (maximum 8 meters).

Air conditioner functions

  • On/Off: press the B1 button to turn the air conditioner on or off.
  • Fan speed Control: press the B2 button to change the speed: Low > Medium > High > Automatic.
  • Automatic mode: automatically adjusts the temperature and fan speed. Press the B5 button until the D5 symbol is displayed.
  • Dehumidification mode: dehumidifies the room. Press the B5 button until the D3 symbol and the minimum ventilation D18 symbol are displayed.
  • Ventilation mode: does not affect temperature or humidity. Press the B5 button until the minimum ventilation D18 symbol is displayed.
  • Heating mode: heats the room. Press the B5 button until the D1 symbol is displayed.
  • Airflow control: press the B4 button to activate/deactivate the continuous oscillation of the movable deflector.
  • SILENT function: gradually reduces the fan speed. Press the B3 button to activate the SILENT function.

Energy saving tips

  • Close doors and windows during use.
  • Do not obstruct the airflow paths.

Diagnostics and alarms

  • LED A: filter cleaning required.
  • LED B: high battery temperature.
  • LED A + C flashing: continuous pump operation.

General warnings

  • Do not manually move the air outlet flap.
  • In case of water leaks, turn off and unplug the appliance immediately.
