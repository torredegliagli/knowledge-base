Toaster instructions

Setting up the toaster

  • Place the toaster on a flat, stable, heat-resistant surface, away from flammable materials such as curtains or shelves.
  • Ensure the crumb tray is properly inserted.

Using the toaster

  • Connecting: plug the toaster into a grounded power outlet.
  • Inserting Bread: insert the slices of bread into the slots.
  • Selecting Browning Level: use the control knob to select the desired browning level.
  • Starting the Toasting Process: press the lever down to start toasting.
  • Stopping the Toaster: stop the toasting at any time by pressing the stop button.

Functions

  • Defrost function: use the defrost function by pressing the designated button before starting the toasting process.
  • Reheat function: use the reheat function by pressing the designated button. The toaster will warm the bread without additional toasting.
  • High lift lever: use the high lift lever to safely remove smaller pieces of bread.

After use

  • The toaster will automatically eject the bread once the cycle is complete.
  • Unplug the toaster when not in use and before cleaning.
  • Allow the toaster to cool before removing and cleaning the crumb tray.
  • Empty the crumb tray regularly to prevent buildup.

Cleaning and maintenance

  • Clean the toaster with a soft cloth. Avoid using abrasive cleaners, steel wool pads, or metal utensils.

Safety precautions

  • Supervise the toaster during use.
  • Do not insert oversized foods, metal utensils, or foil packages into the toaster slots to avoid electric shock or fire.
  • Avoid touching metal parts or hot surfaces during and immediately after use.
  • If the bread gets stuck, unplug the toaster and let it cool before attempting to remove the bread.

Troubleshooting

  • If the bread does not eject or the toaster does not heat, ensure it is properly plugged in and the lever is fully depressed.
  • For any malfunction or if the toaster is not working correctly, unplug it and contact the host, Alessandro.
