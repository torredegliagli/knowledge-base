Neighborhood and surroundings

The Novoli area, while not in the bustling city center, offers a quiet and convenient refuge with a wide variety of local services.

Around the corner

  • Enjoy a nice breakfast at Pasticceria Gaetano just a few meters downstairs, savor the delicious freshly baked products from the bakery La Schiacciateria, and be tempted by the quality meats and tasty ready-to-cook preprations from the butcher Ambrogi. A kebab shop, a small grocery store, and a greengrocer complete the picture of local activities, right around the corner between Via della Torre degli Agli and Via di Novoli.
  • Nearby, there is also a renowned fish restaurant and a traditional street food truck for lampredotto and other varieties of sandwiches, both within walking distance.

San Donato park

  • To relax with a bit of greenery, San Donato Park is just a 5-minute walk away. This park offers large open spaces, ideal for walks, jogging, or simply sitting and enjoying a sunny day.

Esselunga supermarket

  • For larger shopping needs, the Esselunga supermarket is just an 8-minute walk away. Here you will find a well-stocked supermarket for all your food needs, as well as a wide selection of other household products.

San Donato shopping center

  • To relax with some shopping, San Donato shopping center, with The Space cinema and Virgin Active gym, is just a 12-minute walk or 8 minutes by tram. Here you can find various shops, restaurants, and entertainment for a pleasant day out.

Nearest pharmacy

  • The are two pharmacies that are just a 5-minute walk away. Here you can find everything you need for your health and well-being, including over-the-counter medicines, personal care products, and advice from qualified staff.

Street food truck

  • A street food truck, known as 'Il Trippaio', is just a 7-minute walk away. Here you can taste some of the authentic flavors of the Florentine tradition, such as the famous lampredotto, prepared and served fresh every day.

Restaurant Settimo

  • the restaurant Settimo is just a 10-minute walk away. Established in Florence since 1975, this restaurant is renowned for its seafood cuisine with top-quality ingredients and a welcoming atmosphere, perfect for a dinner with traditional Italian sea flavors after a day of exploration.
