User Instructions for Moulinex Kettle

Filling the Kettle

  • Remove the kettle from its base before filling.
  • Fill the kettle with the desired amount of water, ensuring it is between the minimum and maximum levels indicated.
  • Do not overfill as boiling water may spill out.
  • Ensure the lid is properly closed.

Operating the Kettle

  • Place the kettle back on its base and plug it into the mains socket.
  • Press the on/off button located on the handle to start the kettle.
  • The kettle will automatically switch off when the water reaches boiling point or the chosen temperature. You can also stop it manually before removing it from the base to pour the water.

After Use

  • Unplug the kettle and allow it to cool before cleaning or storing.
  • Do not leave water in the kettle after use as this quickens the formation of scale.

Cleaning and Maintenance

  • Unplug the kettle before cleaning.
  • Clean the exterior with a damp sponge. Do not immerse the kettle, its base, the cord, or the electric plug in water.
  • Clean the filter regularly. If the filter is clogged, it can be cleaned under a running tap or with a brush. For descaling, use white vinegar or citric acid as recommended in the manual.

Safety Precautions

  • Do not use the kettle without water to prevent damage.
  • Be cautious of hot steam escaping from the spout.
  • Only touch the kettle by its handle to avoid burns from the hot body.
  • Keep the appliance and its cord out of reach of children under 8 years old.
  • Never open the lid when the water is boiling.
  • Do not use the kettle if the power cord or plug is damaged. Always have them replaced by the manufacturer or a qualified person.

Troubleshooting

  • If the kettle does not work or stops before boiling, check if it is properly connected and ensure there is no scale build-up. Allow the kettle to cool down and refill with water if necessary.
  • For any anomalies or if the kettle does not operate correctly, unplug it and contact the host.
