Check-in instructions

Self Check-in (via Ok Home System)

  • Receive Access: You will receive an email with a link and credentials to access a web app, and a backup code for offline use.
  • Login: Use the provided link and details to log into the web app.


  • Door Control: After logging in for the first time, you will see a welcome page.


  • And then you will be asked to insert your phone number


  • You will finally land on the app Home page. Click on Open Door when you are in proximity of the door to open.


  • You will see icons for three doors: OPEN BUILDING, OPEN DOOR, and OPEN APARTMENT.


  • OPEN BUILDING: It opens the door on the street.
  • OPEN DOOR: It opens the door on the sixth floor (look for the doorbell Nardoni).
  • OPEN APARTMENT: It opens the door of the apartment.
  • Click on the corresponding icon to open the desired door.
  • Notes: The OPEN BUILDING DOOR takes 2-3 seconds to respond after clicking on the smartphone. Do not click too quickly between the OPEN DOOR and the OPEN APARTMENT buttons. Use the app in the proximity of the door only.

Offline Check-in (if the Ok Home System is not available)

  • BUILDING DOOR: Ring the "Nardoni" doorbell X times. This will open the BUILDING DOOR on the street.
  • Access Keys: Once on the 6th floor, use the numeric keypad to open the key box near the door with the doorbell Nardoni.
  • OPEN DOOR: Use the keys from the key box to open the Pianerottolo Door. Return the keys to the key box afterward.
  • APARTMENT DOOR: Use the second numeric keypad and enter the Keypad access code that appears on the Doors page in the web app to open the APARTMENT DOOR.

Important Notes

  • The manual method involves a few extra steps, while the web app is more intuitive and quicker, requiring only a tap on the screen to open the corresponding door. Please always prefer the use of the web app over the offline method.
