User Instructions for Moulinex Toaster

Setting Up the Toaster

  • Place the toaster on a flat, stable, heat-resistant surface away from flammable materials such as curtains or shelves.
  • Ensure the crumb tray is correctly fitted.

Operating the Toaster

  • Plugging In: Plug the toaster into an earthed socket.
  • Inserting Bread: Place the bread slices into the slots.
  • Selecting Browning Level: Use the control knob to select the browning level.
  • Starting the Toasting Process: Press the lever down to start toasting.
  • Stopping the Toaster: Stop the toasting at any time by pressing the stop button.

Functions

  • Defrost Function: Use the defrost function by pressing the designated button before starting the toasting process.
  • Reheat Function: Use the reheat function by pressing the designated button. The toaster will warm the bread without further toasting.
  • High-Lift Lever: Use the high-lift lever to safely remove smaller pieces of bread.

After Use

  • The toaster will automatically pop up the bread once the cycle is complete.
  • Unplug the toaster when not in use and before cleaning.
  • Allow the toaster to cool down before removing and cleaning the crumb tray.
  • Regularly empty the crumb tray to prevent build-up.

Cleaning and Maintenance

  • Clean the toaster with a soft cloth. Avoid using abrasive cleaners, scouring pads, or metal utensils.

Safety Precautions

  • Supervise the toaster while in use.
  • Do not insert oversized foods, metal utensils, or foil packages into the toaster slots to avoid electric shock or fire.
  • Avoid touching the metal parts or hot surfaces during and immediately after use.
  • If the bread gets stuck, unplug the toaster and let it cool before attempting to remove the bread.

Troubleshooting

  • If the bread does not pop up or the toaster does not heat, ensure it is properly plugged in and the lever is fully pressed down.
  • For any anomalies or if the toaster does not operate correctly, unplug it and contact the host.
