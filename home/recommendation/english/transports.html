Getting to the apartment

Below you can find the information on how to get to the apartment and to reach the city centre.

From the Airport

  • Take Tram T2 from Amerigo Vespucci Airport towards Unità. Get off at Novoli - Torre Degli Agli, which is a 10-minute ride with trams running every 6 minutes. From the tram stop, it's a 2-minute walk to Via della Torre degli Agli, 8.



From Santa Maria Novella Train Station

  • From the tram stop Unità, which is near Santa Maria Novella train station, take Tram T2 and get off at Novoli - Torre degli Agli tram stop. The tram ride is about 19 minutes with trams running every 6 minutes. From the tram stop, it's a 2-minute walk to Via della Torre degli Agli, 8.



From the apartment to the City centre

  • From Via della Torre degli Agli, 8, take Tram T2 from Novoli - Torre Degli Agli to Unità, which is near Santa Maria Novella train station. The tram ride is about 19 minutes with trams running every 6 minutes.



Nearest Tram Stop

  • The nearest tram stop is Novoli - Torre Degli Agli. It is only a 2-minute walk from Via della Torre degli Agli, 8. When you exit the tram, walk along Via di Novoli, then turn onto Via della Torre degli Agli.



Car parking (not private)

  • Free public parking is available on Via della Torre degli Agli and surrounding streets. Please note that parking spaces are subject to availability and may be limited during peak hours. Always check for any parking restrictions or signs.


Notes

  • The apartment offers a strategic base for exploring the city.
  • It is a 2-minute walk from the Novoli - Torre degli Agli tram stop, which is on the T2 tram line that links the Airport to the City centre and Santa Maria Novella train station.
  • 10 minutes to the airport.
  • 19 minutes to Santa Maria Novella train station.
  • 19 minutes to the city center.
