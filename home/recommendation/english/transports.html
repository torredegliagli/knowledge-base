Public Transport Information

Airport - Via Torre degli Agli, 8

  • Take Tram T2 from Amerigo Vespucci Airport towards Unità. Get off at Novoli - Torre Degli Agli, which is a 10-minute ride with trams running every 6 minutes. From the tram stop, it's a 2-minute walk to Via della Torre degli Agli, 8.



Nearest Tram Stop - Via Torre degli Agli, 8

  • The nearest tram stop is Novoli - Torre Degli Agli. It is only a 2-minute walk from Via della Torre degli Agli, 8. When you exit the tram, walk along Via di Novoli, then turn onto Via della Torre degli Agli.



City Centre/Train Station - Via Torre degli Agli, 8

  • From Via della Torre degli Agli, 8, take Tram T2 from Novoli - Torre Degli Agli to Unità, which is near the city center and the main train station. The tram ride is about 19 minutes with trams running every 6 minutes.



Car parking (not private) - Via Torre degli Agli, 8

  • Free public parking is available on Via della Torre degli Agli and surrounding streets. Please note that parking spaces are subject to availability and may be limited during peak hours. Always check for any parking restrictions or signs.
