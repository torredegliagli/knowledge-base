House Rules

  • Check-in: after 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: by 10:00 AM
  • No Smoking: Do not smoke inside the apartment.
  • Pets: Unfortunately, pets are not allowed.
  • Quiet Hours: Please respect our neighbors by keeping noise to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.
  • Safety Measures: Make sure all appliances are turned off, windows are not wide open, and the door is locked when you leave.
  • Waste Disposal: Separate waste and use the provided bins. You will need an electronic key (provided) to use the external bins on the street.
  • Damage: Inform us immediately if something is damaged. You are responsible for any damage during your stay.
  • Maximum Occupancy: Up to 4 guests are allowed.
  • No Parties: Parties or events are not allowed.
  • Visitors: Visitors are welcome but overnight stays are not permitted.
  • Parking: There is no reserved parking, but you can usually find a spot on Via della Torre degli Agli. Use these coordinates: 43°47'42.5"N 11°13'09.3"E.
  • Kitchen Use: Clean up after using the kitchen. Do not leave dirty dishes or perishable food, and make sure all appliances are turned off when you are finished.
  • Bathroom Use: Keep the bathroom clean. Only flush toilet paper and report any plumbing issues immediately.
  • Washing Machine Use: Follow the instructions for using the washing machine. Do not overload it and remove laundry promptly to avoid mold.
  • Emergency Contacts:
    • 24h Emergency number: 112
    • English-speaking medical services: +39 320 406 5709, doctorsinitaly.com
    • 24-hour Pharmacy (Farmacia Santa Maria Novella): Via della Scala, 19, +39 055 216761
  • Wi-Fi Use and internet: Please avoid illegal activities online and any illegal use of the information provided on the apartment website.
  • Local Laws: Follow all local laws and regulations.
  • Respect for Property: Treat the apartment and its contents with respect.
  • Lost and Found: Contact us immediately if you find or lose personal items.
